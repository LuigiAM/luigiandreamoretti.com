Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how luigiandreamoretti.com ("the Website") collects, uses, and protects information about visitors. I am committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about data practices.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Information You Provide

The Website does not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it by:

  • Sending an email to hello@luigimoretti.com
  • Clicking external links to contact me via LinkedIn or other platforms

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The Website may automatically collect non-personal technical information through:

  • Server Logs: IP addresses, browser types, device information, pages visited, timestamps
  • Cloudflare Analytics: Aggregated traffic statistics (no cookies, no personal data tracking)

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 Google Search Console

This Website is registered with Google Search Console for performance monitoring and indexing optimization. Google may collect:

  • Page URLs and crawl statistics
  • Search query data (when users find the site via Google)
  • Click-through rates from search results

No personally identifiable information is collected through Search Console. Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

2.2 YouTube Embedded Videos

The Website embeds YouTube videos (MEMoPAD project demos). When you play a video, YouTube may collect:

  • Viewing statistics
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address (for video delivery)

YouTube's data practices: YouTube Privacy Policy

2.3 External Links

The Website contains links to external sites (LinkedIn, GitHub, ResearchGate, MEMoPAD project site). I am not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services.

3. Cookies

This Website does not use cookies.

Third-party services (YouTube, LinkedIn) may set cookies when you interact with embedded content or click external links. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. Data Usage

Information collected is used solely for:

  • Website performance optimization
  • Understanding visitor behavior (aggregated, non-personal)
  • Responding to inquiries sent via email

I do not:

  • Sell or share personal data with third parties
  • Use data for advertising or marketing
  • Track individual users across sessions

5. Data Security

The Website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages with:

  • SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS)
  • DDoS protection
  • Secure server infrastructure

While I implement reasonable security measures, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR:

  • Access: Request information about data collected
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Objection: Object to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact me at hello@luigimoretti.com.

7. Children's Privacy

This Website is not intended for individuals under 16 years of age. I do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
